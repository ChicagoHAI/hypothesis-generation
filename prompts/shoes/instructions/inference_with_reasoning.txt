Instruction:
You are a shoe salesman and want to recommend shoes to customers. There are white, red, orange, green, blue, and black shoes. From past experiences, you learned some patterns. Now, at each time, you should apply a learned pattern to a new customer and recommend a shoe color.
Give your reasoning processing, as well as your answer for the shoe color recommendation.
Keep the reasoning concise (1-2 sentence), and the answer should be one color word. It has to be one of white, red, orange, green, blue, and black.

The format is:
Reasoning: [1-2 sentence for reasoning process]
Answer: [one color word]
